Interpreting the Theme

The Team:

Josh Mahan, Cristina Keane: Designers
Jon Del Sola, James Matthews : Developers
Chris Kula: Audio Production


The Global Game Jam occurs annually in cities all around the world from Paris to Los Angeles. It encourages the indie game community to build their ideas based on the theme provided. Blackout Bunny won the "Best Interpretation of Theme" award.

"2014 GGJ THEME: We don't see things as they are, we see them as we are."
